Tips before buying gold

  • Always ensure to buy only hallmarked jewelery and with Proper bill/documentation

  • Get your gold checked in caratometer in front of your eyes

  • Explore the major jewelery brands and ask for discounts/schemes available, Try to give as less making charges as possible

  • Try to buy gold from trusted jewelery shops as it is huge investment of hard earned money

  • Generally all big brands like Tanishq, Malabar, Kalyan all have monthly schemes which can help you buy gold with monthly installments. Some of them also offer discount in the making charges in such schemes.

  • Ensure you have read all the terms and conditions of such schemes. We are not liable for any of your loses
